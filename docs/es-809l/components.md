# ส่วนประกอบ

## ตัวล็อกด้านนอก

| # | ชิ้นส่วน | หน้าที่ |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Number Pad** | The pin number is entered here to register or to open the door |
| 2 | **RFID Card Sensor** | It is the sensor where card is placed when opening the door or during card registration |
| 3 | **Battery Lamp** | When the battery is low it turns on along with the alarm |
| 4 | **Mechanical key Hole cover** | Protects the emergency mechanical key hole |
| 5 | **Mechanical key Hole** | In case of emergency the mechanical key can be inserted here to open the door |
| 6 | **Outbody handle** | It is used when opening or closing the door from outside |
| 7 | **9V Battery Terminal** | When the battery is completely discharged. Operate the lock using a 9V battery. |
| 8 | **Reset Switch** | When the product is not functioning normally, initialize the lock through the Reset switch |

## ตัวล็อกด้านใน

| # | ชิ้นส่วน | หน้าที่ |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Battery Cover** | This is the cover that protects the battery and registration button |
| 2 | **Registration Button** | This is used when registering Pin & Card |
| 3 | **Safety button** | When enabled will not allow opening of door from outside either with Pin or Card |
| 4 | **Mortise** | Is mounted at the side of the lock. It is the main locking device. |
| 5 | **Deadbolt** | It is the locking mechanism |
| 6 | **Latch Bolt** | It fix the door in place |
| 7 | **Alkaline AA Battery** | Is the main power supply of the lock |
| 8 | **Manual Lock Button** | It is used to close the door in manual locking mode |
| 9 | **Inbody Handle** | It is used to open or close the door from the inside |
