# Outer Body Cable Management Guide

This guide highlights the critical warnings and proper techniques for routing the Outer Body connection cable inside the Inner Body housing during lock installation.

## ⚠️ Warnings on Incorrect Cable Routing

:::caution[Correct Cable Management]
- When organizing the Outer Body connection cable inside the Inner Body plate, **ensure the cable is laid out cleanly** without obstruction.
- **Why it matters:** If the cable is routed in an **Incorrect Way**, it can be pinched or press against the lock mechanism and drive motor of the Inner Body. This pressure can impede movement, cause mechanical locking errors, overheat the motor, or lead to complete door lock malfunctions.
- Please refer to the correct routing diagrams (**Correct Way**) in your installation sheet and exercise extreme caution when securing the fixing screws.
:::
