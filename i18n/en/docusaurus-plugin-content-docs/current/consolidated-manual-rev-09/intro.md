## ✨ Key Features

- **Dual Authentication** — Once enabled, requires PIN + (Card or Fingerprint)
- **Internal / External Forced Lock** — Forced lock from inside/outside
- **Smart Card Auto Recognition** — RFID cards auto-recognized
- **Random Number Feature** — Add random digits before/after PIN to prevent shoulder-surfing
- **1-Minute Lock Warning** — Locks for 1 minute after 5 failed attempts
- **Low Battery Warning** — Alerts when battery is low
- **Automatic Re-locking** — Auto-locks after door closes
- **Deadbolt Error Warning** — Alerts on deadbolt malfunction
- **Intrusion Alarm** — Anti-tamper alarm
