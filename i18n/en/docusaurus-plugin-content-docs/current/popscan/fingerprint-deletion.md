# Fingerprint Deletion

## Delete all

1. Open the battery cover. Press **Registration** once.
2. Enter your PIN, then press `*`.
3. Press number `9`.
4. Hold `*` for 5 seconds. When you hear a melody, all fingerprints have been deleted.
